About these instructions

I am a self taught artist of almost 40 years and have developed my own rendering techniques that combine pencil art with colored pencil and marker. Using photoshop and a printer I can take refined drawings, resize them and composite them with other drawings, then print them and render them in full color. This is very useful in graphic design to combine hand drawn and computer generated imagery/photography. It is my primary way of creating images for a graphic novel I am working on, and

I am currently using the technique for the web page design for my wife’s podcast about Native American representation in romance novels entitled “The Novel Savage.” This will be eventually found at Inappropriations.com, if we ever finish it.
